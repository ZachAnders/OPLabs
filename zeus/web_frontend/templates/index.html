Curious about the speed of your home network?


Hermes is a new application that tests the quality of your Wifi connection within your home by comparing the speed of your phone running tests to your router running tests. If your internet provider has set you up with a router that supports Hermes, follow these simple steps to get started today!

Step 1: Create an Account


Hermes requires you to create an account so that your router settings can be remembered, and so that unique historical results can be provided to you. The email you provide will only be used in cases of password reset.

Step 2: Download the Mobile App


Install our mobile applications and run speedtests from your own phone. Available on the iOS App Store and Android Play Store. Use the account you create here to login on the app.
